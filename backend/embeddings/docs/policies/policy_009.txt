Title: Data Retention and Documentation Policy
Effective Date: February 1, 2024

Purpose
To ensure proper storage and retention of corporate records.

Scope
All departments.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Financial records retained for 7 years.
2. CRM logs retained for minimum 3 years.
3. Audit documents securely archived.
4. Deletion requires Legal approval.

Escalation and Compliance
Non-compliance may result in audit findings.
