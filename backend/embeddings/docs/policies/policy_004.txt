Title: CRM Data Entry Policy
Effective Date: March 1, 2024

Purpose
To ensure accurate, timely, and standardized entry of data into the CRM system to support operational efficiency and reporting integrity.

Scope
All departments utilizing CRM, including Sales, Customer Success, and Finance.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. All client interactions must be logged within 24 hours.
2. Invoice references must match ERP records.
3. Duplicate entries must be avoided; suspected duplicates require review.
4. Mandatory fields must not be bypassed.
5. CRM outages must be reported to IT within one hour.
6. Manual spreadsheets should not replace CRM entries except under documented exceptions.

Escalation and Compliance
Non-compliance may result in performance review implications. IT will audit CRM logs quarterly.
