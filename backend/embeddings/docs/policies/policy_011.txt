Title: SLA Enforcement Policy
Effective Date: January 20, 2024

Purpose
To ensure contractual SLA commitments are met.

Scope
Operations, Customer Support, Finance.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. SLA breaches logged in CRM.
2. Compensation decisions per Refund Policy.
3. Vendor SLA breaches escalated to Procurement.
4. Quarterly SLA performance review required.

Escalation and Compliance
Repeated breaches require executive review.
