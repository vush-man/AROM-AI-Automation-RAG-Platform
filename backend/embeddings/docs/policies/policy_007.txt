Title: Refund and Reimbursement Policy
Effective Date: January 5, 2024

Purpose
To define consistent processes for issuing customer refunds and employee reimbursements.

Scope
All departments managing financial disbursements.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Refund requests require documented proof of eligibility.
2. Finance must process approved refunds within 7 business days.
3. Reimbursements require original receipts.
4. Exceptions require Finance Controller approval.
5. Disputed cases must be documented.

Escalation and Compliance
Repeated refund delays must be escalated to CFO.
