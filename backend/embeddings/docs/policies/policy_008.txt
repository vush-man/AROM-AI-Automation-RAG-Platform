Title: Financial Approval Hierarchy Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Purpose
To define authorization thresholds for financial commitments.

Scope
All departments.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Managers may approve up to $10,000.
2. Directors up to $50,000.
3. CFO approval required beyond $50,000.
4. Emergency approvals require written justification.
5. All approvals must be recorded electronically.

Escalation and Compliance
Unauthorized commitments may lead to disciplinary action.
