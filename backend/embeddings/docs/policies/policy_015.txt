Title: Risk and Exception Handling Policy
Effective Date: January 25, 2024

Purpose
To define structured risk identification and exception management processes.

Scope
All departments.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Risks must be documented with mitigation plans.
2. Exceptions to policies require written approval.
3. High-risk exceptions require executive review.
4. Risk register maintained quarterly.

Escalation and Compliance
Failure to document risks may result in compliance findings.
