Title: Escalation Management Policy
Effective Date: January 10, 2024

Purpose
To define standardized procedures for escalating operational, financial, or customer-related issues requiring urgent attention.

Scope
Applies to all departments.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Issues classified as “High” must be escalated within 24 hours.
2. Escalation documentation must include summary, impact, and recommended action.
3. Finance-related escalations must include invoice ID or transaction reference.
4. Escalations should follow defined reporting lines.
5. Repeated escalations on the same issue require management review.

Escalation and Compliance
Improper or excessive escalation may be reviewed by department leadership. Compliance with this policy is mandatory.
