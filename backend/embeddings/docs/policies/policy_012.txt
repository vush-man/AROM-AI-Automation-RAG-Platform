Title: Procurement Policy
Effective Date: February 15, 2024

Purpose
To govern purchasing activities.

Scope
All departments.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Purchases require approved PO.
2. Competitive bidding required for >$25,000.
3. Vendor selection must align with Vendor Onboarding Policy.
4. Emergency purchases require documentation.

Escalation and Compliance
Unauthorized purchases subject to review.
