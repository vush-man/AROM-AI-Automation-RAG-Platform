Title: Inter-Department Communication Policy
Effective Date: March 1, 2024

Purpose
To standardize communication across departments.

Scope
All employees.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Financial queries must include invoice ID.
2. Escalations require summary and timeline.
3. Meeting minutes must be circulated within 48 hours.
4. Email CC usage should be limited to relevant stakeholders.

Escalation and Compliance
Chronic miscommunication may be addressed through HR review.
