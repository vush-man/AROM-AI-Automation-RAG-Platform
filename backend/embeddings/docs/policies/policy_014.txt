Title: Manual Data Handling Guidelines
Effective Date: March 10, 2024

Purpose
To regulate manual data entry and reduce processing risk.

Scope
All employees handling financial or CRM data.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Manual entries must be logged.
2. Dual verification required for financial data.
3. Temporary spreadsheets must be archived.
4. Automation initiatives encouraged.

Escalation and Compliance
Excessive manual reliance subject to audit review.
