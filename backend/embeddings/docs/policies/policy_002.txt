Title: Vendor Onboarding Policy
Effective Date: February 1, 2024

Purpose
The Vendor Onboarding Policy establishes a structured process for evaluating, approving, and registering new vendors. This ensures legal compliance, risk mitigation, and alignment with corporate procurement standards.

Scope
This policy applies to Procurement, Finance, Legal, and all departments initiating vendor relationships.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. All new vendors must complete a standardized onboarding form including tax identification, banking details, and compliance certifications.
2. Procurement shall conduct due diligence, including background verification and conflict-of-interest checks.
3. Vendors must acknowledge acceptance of the Vendor SLA Policy and Payment Processing Policy 2024.
4. Finance shall verify banking information and ensure compliance with anti-fraud controls.
5. Legal review is required for contracts exceeding $50,000 annually.
6. Vendor records must be entered into the CRM and ERP systems prior to any payment release.
7. Changes to vendor information require documented approval and audit trail retention.

Escalation and Compliance
Exceptions require written approval from the Head of Procurement. Failure to comply may result in suspension of vendor registration or payment processing delays.
