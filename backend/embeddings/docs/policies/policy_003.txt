Title: Invoice Approval Workflow Policy
Effective Date: January 15, 2024

Purpose
This policy defines the structured approval workflow for all invoices to ensure transparency, accountability, and financial accuracy.

Scope
Applies to all departments submitting invoices for payment.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Invoices must be matched to valid POs before approval.
2. Department managers must approve invoices within five business days of receipt.
3. Invoices above $25,000 require secondary approval from Finance.
4. Automated workflows must be used; manual approvals require justification.
5. Delays beyond ten business days must be reported to Finance.
6. Disputed invoices must be flagged and documented in CRM.

Escalation and Compliance
Failure to follow approval timelines may be escalated to department heads. Repeated violations may trigger audit review.
