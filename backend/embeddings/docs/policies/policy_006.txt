Title: Complaint Resolution Policy
Effective Date: February 10, 2024

Purpose
To provide structured guidelines for handling customer complaints in a timely and professional manner.

Scope
Applies to Customer Support, Sales, and Operations.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Complaints must be logged in CRM within 12 hours.
2. Initial acknowledgment to customer within one business day.
3. Complex complaints requiring cross-department action must follow Escalation Management Policy.
4. Refunds must align with Refund and Reimbursement Policy.
5. Resolution summaries must be documented.

Escalation and Compliance
Unresolved complaints beyond 10 days require management review.
