Title: Audit and Compliance Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Purpose
To define internal audit processes.

Scope
All departments.

Detailed Rules and Procedures

1. Quarterly financial audits conducted.
2. CRM compliance reviews performed biannually.
3. Audit findings documented and tracked.
4. Corrective actions required within 30 days.

Escalation and Compliance
Non-remediation may escalate to Board review.
